FAQ

Answers to the questions leaders ask us first

Before the first call, here’s how Pediment typically engages — scope, team model, and what to expect.

What does Pediment specialise in?
We focus on strategy, operations, and digital transformation for mid-market and enterprise leadership teams — pairing senior advisors with delivery teams that stay until results show up.
Where are you based and where do you deliver?
Partners are based in London and New York, with delivery across 18 countries. Engagements run on-site, remote, or hybrid depending on the work.
What does a typical engagement look like?
Most engagements run around 14 weeks: a diagnose phase grounded in your data, a design phase with clear trade-offs, then embedded delivery alongside your team.
How is Pediment different from a traditional consultancy?
No deck-and-leave. Senior people stay hands-on through delivery, and we transfer capability so the results outlast the engagement.
Who are your engagements best suited for?
Leadership teams navigating growth, cost pressure, or transformation — typically €100M+ organisations where the decision genuinely matters.
